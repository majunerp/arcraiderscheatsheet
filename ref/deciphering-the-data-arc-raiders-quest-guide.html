To complete Deciphering the Data in ARC Raiders, you’ll need to visit Spaceport and use a Magnetic Decryptor in the Fuel Control Building. After the Decryptor breaks, you must head to the Arrival Building and find another Decryptor on the top floor to finish the job.

The “Deciphering the Data” quest becomes available after completing “Paving the Way.” It sends you back to Spaceport to interact with specific computer terminals. These buildings are often hot zones for PvP, so knowing exactly where to look is key to surviving. Here is a quick walkthrough.

Deciphering the Data ARC Raiders Quest Overview:

Quest Giver: Shani

Objectives:

  • Use the Magnetic Decryptor in the Fuel Control Building
  • Reach the Arrival Building in Spaceport
  • Use the Magnetic Decryptor on the top floor of the Arrival Building

Rewards:

  • Defibrillator x3
  • Trailblazer Grenade x3

Step 1: The Fuel Control Building

Your first target is the Fuel Control Building, located in the Northeast section of the Spaceport map (right next to Rocket Assembly).

  • The Location: Enter the main building. It is very dark inside, so you may need to toggle your flashlight.
  • The Decryptor: Look for a server room area. Against the Southeast wall, you will find a distinct blue terminal/machine that looks different from the others.
  • Action: Interact with it to attempt decryption. Shani will tell you it failed and direct you to a new location.

Step 2: The Arrival Building

Now you need to cross the map to the Northwest side to reach the Arrival Building. Be careful crossing the open tarmac, as snipers often watch from the towers and Rocketeers patrol the crossing!

  • The Location: Enter the Arrival Building and head straight for the stairs. Go all the way up to the Top Floor.
  • The Decryptor: In the middle of the main hall/corridor, look for two long rows of ancient computer banks. One of these machines is the second Magnetic Decryptor.
  • Action: Interact with the machine to successfully decipher the data.

Step 3: Extract from Deciphering the Data ARC Raiders Quest (Optional)

Once you interact with the second machine, the quest is complete! You can now extract to secure any loot you found along the way. Don’t forget to return to Shani in Speranza to hand the quest in for the Rewards.

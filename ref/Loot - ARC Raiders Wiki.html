Loot are items that can be collected in the world of ARC Raiders. These items are often needed for tasks such as Crafting recipes, Quests, Workshop Upgrades.

You can recycle almost every item for basic materials, but recycling can only be done in Speranza. In-raid, you can instead salvage the item to yield fewer or lower-quality items.

Use the categorized tables below to prioritize what to take and what to leave behind.
